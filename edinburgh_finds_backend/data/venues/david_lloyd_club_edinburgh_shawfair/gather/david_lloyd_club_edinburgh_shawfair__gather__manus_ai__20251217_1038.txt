Core Facilities & Amenities
The club offers a comprehensive range of facilities, as detailed in official publications [1][3].
Category
Details
Gym
A 100+ station gym featuring a wide array of cardio, free weights, cable machines, and functional training equipment. It includes a dedicated stretching zone and the "Intuitive Strength Circuit" for personalized workouts [1][3].
Swimming Pools
A 20-meter heated indoor pool and a 25-meter heated outdoor pool. The club provides aqua aerobics, family swim times, and adult-only sessions [1][3].
Spa Retreat
An extensive indoor and outdoor spa area, the "Spa Retreat" and "Spa Garden," includes Himalayan salt saunas, aromatherapy steam rooms, a hydrotherapy pool, heated beds, an ice cave, and a plunge pool [1][3].
Racquet Sports
The venue is a significant racquets center, featuring 12 indoor and 7 outdoor tennis courts (including a tennis dome for year-round play ), 3 outdoor Padel courts, and facilities for badminton and pickleball [1].
Group Fitness
Four bespoke studios offer a variety of classes, including high-intensity BLAZE and Battlebox sessions, Cyclone (group cycling), and mind-body classes like SPIRIT, Yoga, and Pilates [1][3].
Family & Kids
Dedicated "DL Kids" area with activities, holiday clubs, and coaching for swimming and tennis. A crèche is also available. The Clubroom includes a dedicated family area with an indoor playframe [1][3].
Social & Work
A multi-zone Clubroom serves restaurant-quality meals and includes a business lounge for remote work. An outdoor patio with a playground is available for dining and socializing [1][3].
