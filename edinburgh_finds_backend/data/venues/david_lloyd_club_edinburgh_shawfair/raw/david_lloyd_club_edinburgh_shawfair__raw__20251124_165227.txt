VENUE NAME
David Lloyd Club Edinburgh Shawfair
Overview
David Lloyd Club Edinburgh Shawfair is a health, fitness, and wellness facility that opened in August 2023. The club features over 100 workout stations in its gym, four bespoke exercise studios, indoor and outdoor heated swimming pools, and extensive racquet sports facilities including tennis, padel, and pickleball courts. The venue includes a spa retreat with hot and cold therapies, a clubroom for dining and socializing, and family-friendly amenities including a crèche and children's programs.
Address & Location
Old Dalkeith Road, Danderhall, Dalkeith, EH22 1FD. The club is located to the south-east of Edinburgh in the Shawfair development area in Midlothian, Scotland.

Facilities & Amenities
The club has a 250-space car park with disabled and parent-child spaces. It features a clubroom café bar with family zone, business lounge and workspace, indoor soft play area, and outdoor patio. Additional amenities include Wi-Fi access and a dedicated marketing suite.
Gym Facilities
The gym contains over 100 workout stations with modern cardio machines, strength training equipment, and free weights. Equipment includes EGYM Smart Strength machines, Watt bikes, and incline treadmills. A dedicated stretching and recovery zone with specialized equipment is available, along with a Body Hub for tracking health metrics like hydration levels and muscle mass.
Swimming Facilities
The club has a 20-metre indoor heated pool and a 25-metre outdoor heated pool. Activities include aqua aerobics, family swim times, quiet adult swim sessions, and swimming lessons for adults and children. Year-round swimming is available in both pools.
Spa & Wellness
The Spa Retreat features a heated spa pool, Himalayan salt saunas, crystal steam rooms, heated beds, plunge pool, and ice cave. An outdoor Spa Garden includes sauna, spa bath, fire pit, and relaxation loungers. The spa offers aromatherapy steam rooms and scented spa showers for post-workout recovery.
Racquet Sports
The club has twelve indoor tennis courts and seven outdoor tennis courts, plus three outdoor padel courts and facilities for badminton and pickleball. Four tennis courts are housed inside an aerodome for all-weather play. Expert coaches provide introductory, beginner, and refresher courses, along with club championships, weekly tournaments, and social events.
Classes & Coaching
Over 100 group exercise classes are offered weekly, including HIIT (Blaze), indoor cycling (Cyclone), Pilates, yoga, and mind-body classes (SPIRIT). Classes are bookable through the club app. Personal training services are available at an additional cost. Tennis and swimming coaching programs are provided for both adults and children.
Family & Children
A crèche staffed by qualified personnel is available for younger members. Children's programs include swimming lessons, tennis coaching starting from age 3 months to 18 years, daily activities, holiday clubs, and arts and crafts programs including Lego Squad. The clubroom includes a dedicated family area with playframe and a special children's menu.
Food & Drink
The clubroom serves restaurant-quality meals with light bites and substantial dishes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Vegan and vegetarian options are available along with freshly blended smoothies. Coffee is provided by Change Please, a social enterprise supporting people experiencing homelessness. Orders can be placed through the club app for table delivery.
Parking & Transport
The club has a 250-space car park. Public transport access includes buses 33, 48, 51, 200, 31, X18, and X38 to nearby stops. Shawfair railway station on the Borders Railway is approximately 1 mile north of the club. The club is well-connected to Edinburgh and surrounding towns in Midlothian and East Lothian by bus, rail, and cycle links.
Accessibility
The car park includes designated disabled parking spaces. A ticketing system for disabled parking was introduced following member feedback. None found regarding additional accessibility features.
Reviews & Reputation
The club holds a 4.6 star rating based on 368 reviews. Members praised the spacious facilities, modern equipment, friendly staff, and supportive community. Some complaints emerged in early 2024 regarding overcrowding during peak times, parking availability, and class booking challenges during busy periods. The club introduced a waiting list to manage capacity and added extra group classes in response.
Staff & Management
Michael Lindores was appointed as General Manager, transferring from David Lloyd Aberdeen to oversee the club opening in 2023. Michelle Chambers-Cran serves as Regional Manager for David Lloyd Leisure overseeing the Edinburgh area. The club generated approximately 75 jobs when operational and employs staff across hospitality, spa, fitness, and reception departments.
Membership & Pricing
Membership types include Individual, Couple, and Family options across Club, Plus, and Platinum tiers. Pricing varies by membership level, with estimates around £75-£125 monthly for individual memberships based on tier selection. Supplementary services including personal training, crèche, and specialized coaching are available at additional cost.
Additional information not included in headings
The club opened following planning approval granted by Midlothian Council in April 2021. Construction began in October 2022 and generated over £4 million for local subcontractors and 50-60 construction jobs. The development aligns with masterplan principles for Shawfair to encourage sustainable, healthy lifestyles within a vibrant community.
