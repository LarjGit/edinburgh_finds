Membership at David Lloyd Edinburgh Shawfair
--------------------------------------------

As a David Lloyd Clubs member you'll be part of a supportive community with access to exceptional spaces, experiences and everything you need to live well.

### Membership options

Depending on the membership type you choose, you’ll have use of the gym, swimming pool, spa, racquets courts, exercise classes and Clubroom. Plus, to help you get the most from your membership, we also offer supplementary services such as the onsite crèche, kids swimming and tennis coaching and personal training, available at an additional cost. Take a look at the different types of [membership on offer](https://www.davidlloyd.co.uk/memberships/) to find the best package for you.

### Spa experiences

At David Lloyd Edinburgh Shawfair, every moment is designed to enhance your overall wellbeing. Step into the [Spa Retreat and the Spa Garden](https://www.davidlloyd.co.uk/clubs/edinburgh-shawfair/club-facilities/spa-and-wellbeing/) — serene sanctuaries created to calm the mind and restore the body. Immerse yourself in the soothing warmth of indoor and outdoor saunas, bespoke aromatherapy steam rooms, and revitalising plunge pools, all thoughtfully curated to elevate your recovery and relaxation.

### Exercise classes

Carefully designed and curated, our group exercise programme offers a huge range of classes, including HIIT, yoga, cycling, Pilates, and cardio. Or, try something completely new with our exclusive Signature classes like BLAZE, our ultimate immersive workout. Whatever your preferred class — and whatever your fitness goals — we have something for you. [Take a look at our timetable here.](https://www.davidlloyd.co.uk/clubs/edinburgh-shawfair/timetable/)

### Racquet courts and coaching

With twelve indoor and seven outdoor tennis courts, plus badminton, Padel and Pickleball facilities, David Lloyd Edinburgh Shawfair is a first-rate racquets venue. Our expert coaches offer introductory, beginner and refresher courses for players of all abilities. We also have a thriving racquets community with something to offer every player, from Club championships to weekly tournaments and social events. [Explore tennis and racquets here](https://www.davidlloyd.co.uk/clubs/edinburgh-shawfair/club-facilities/racquets/).

Play Padel in Edinburgh Shawfair
--------------------------------

Padel is fast-paced, fun, and easy to pick up making it perfect for all ages and abilities. Played in doubles on a smaller, enclosed court, it blends the best of tennis and squash with quick rallies and plenty of action. Whether you’re a seasoned player or brand new to the game, it’s easy to get started and hard to stop playing.
